About Our Clinic

We are dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services to our community with compassion and expertise.

Compassionate Care Since 2005

MediCare Family Clinic was founded with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to families in our community. For over 18 years, we've been committed to improving the health and well-being of our patients through personalized care.

Our team of experienced healthcare professionals stays current with the latest medical advancements to ensure you receive the best possible treatment in a warm and welcoming environment.

We believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms, and take the time to listen to your concerns and answer your questions.
